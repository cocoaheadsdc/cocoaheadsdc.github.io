Sean Soper, iOS technical architect at The Washington Post, will talk to us about the powerful yet rarely used Accelerate framework. Common operations such as image manipulation can easily see a 2x or 4x speedup with some vectorization and a little math.

Please note March's meet-up will be held on the second Thursday of the month at The Washington Post. Pizza and refreshments will be provided courtesy of The Washington Post.

100% Pure Geek

No, we are not in the chocolate business. We are, however, obsessed with Cocoa – Apple’s programming framework for Mac OS X including the iPhone. We also delve into other Apple-related technologies such as Swift and mobile Safari. Our meetings are held on the first Thursday of every month where members present projects and tutorials. You should join us!
